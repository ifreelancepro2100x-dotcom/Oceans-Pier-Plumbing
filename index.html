Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what Oceanside homeowners ask us most.

How fast can you get to my home in Oceanside?

For emergency calls within Oceanside city limits, we typically arrive within 45–60 minutes. Our 5 service vans are stationed across the area — from Downtown to Fire Mountain to South O — so help is never far away. Non-emergency appointments can usually be scheduled same-day or next-day.

Do you charge extra for nights, weekends, or holidays?

No. Unlike most plumbing companies, Ocean Pier Plumbing charges the same flat rate 24/7/365. Whether you call at noon Tuesday or 3 AM Christmas morning, our pricing stays the same — because plumbing emergencies don't follow business hours.

Are you licensed and insured in California?

Yes — we hold a California C-36 Plumbing Contractor License, plus ASSE Backflow Prevention Certification, Medical Gas Certification (ASSE 6010), and Green Plumbing Certification. We carry General Liability, Commercial Auto, Workers' Compensation, and Tool Coverage insurance. Documentation available upon request.

Why does my older Oceanside home need repiping?

Many homes in neighborhoods like Eastside, Plumosa Heights, and North Oceanside Terrace were built with galvanized steel pipes that corrode from the inside out — and Oceanside's salty coastal air accelerates the process. Signs you need repiping include low water pressure, discolored water, frequent leaks, and pipes over 50 years old. We offer free in-home repiping consultations.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Absolutely. All our labor is warrantied, and we pass through manufacturer warranties on every fixture and appliance we install. Water heater installations include extended warranty options, and major projects like repiping come with multi-year guarantees. Ask your technician for specifics on your job.
